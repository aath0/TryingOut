These are some of the research questions that keep me busy:

How do we extract regularities from our environment? In our everyday lives we are constantly exposed to streams of sensory stimuli such as images, speech or music. Most of the time, these do not occur in a random way but follow statistical rules and repetitive patterns. My goal is to understand how we detect such rules and construct models of our environment.

How is our perception influenced by attention or awareness? Perception of our environment may occur explicitly, as for example when we try to learn a new song, or implicitly, when for example we recognise a song while our attention is diverted to other tasks. I am interested in studying which brain functions enable learning as a function of attention or awareness.

And these are some of the techniques that I'm using to answer them:
